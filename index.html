About

The Whale and the Porpoise is an experimental long-form musical improvisation group based out of Cambridge, MA. The show begins with a single suggestion from the audience. Then, right before your eyes, we will turn your suggestion into a brand new musical, complete with improvised songs and choreography!

We enjoy playing with a variety of formats; currently we perform the Harold, which is a classic long-form improv structure to which we've added a twist of musicality. We are directed by Lauren Robinson and musically directed by Micha Goolsby.
