About

The Whale and the Porpoise is an experimental long-form musical improvisation group. It all starts with one suggestion from the audience, then we create a series of seemingly unrelated comedic and dramatic sketches on the spot, complete with improvised songs and choreography. By the end of the show, the scenes may (or may not) tie together in some way.
