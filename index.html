About

The Whale and the Porpoise is an experimental long-form musical improvisation group. The show begins when you, an audience member tells us a story from your life. Then, right before your eyes, we will turn your life into a brand new musical number, complete with improvised songs and choreography!

We are proud to be a part of ImprovBoston's Studio 40, a select group of independent improv troupes from the Boston area.
